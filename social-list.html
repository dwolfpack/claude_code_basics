Social Impact

The projects you build are only half the story. These guides show you how to present your work online so future employers can find it, understand it, and remember it.

Why this matters

Recruiters and hiring managers search LinkedIn and GitHub before they ever look at a CV. A well-optimised profile, a clear project README, and one well-written post about something you built can open doors that a traditional job application never will. These guides give you the exact playbook — backed by research and ready-made Claude prompts to write the copy.
